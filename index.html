Game addiction phases

Starting phase

So you found or heard about a new game, maybe from a friend? you like the idea so just try the game and you like it and play it a little

phase 2 (the beginning of addiction)

So you liked the game a little bit more and oh! well, i guess you forgot you homework just because you were playing. That's new, but you don't think much of it

phase 3 (the beginning of problems)

So you started to play more and more and you started to forget about your homework, your family, your self and your friends are pixels to you. You played more and more games and started leaving everything aside just to play, BUT you are starting to feel you aren't the same; Your grades aren't the same, your mental health, your body and even your relationship with people around you is getting more and more distanced

Phase 4 (the beginning of the end)

So you are now in the last phase of addiction, you are now in a point where you can't stop playing, you can't stop thinking about the game, you can't stop thinking about the next level, the next mission, the next thing. You are now in a point where you are losing your life, your friends, your family, your health and even your self, but now you are feeling a lot worse that you want to change so badly, you are feeling so desperate that you want to end it all.

Phase 5 (the awakening)

So you are now in the last phase of addiction, you are now in a point where you still can't stop playing but you don't want to play anymore. It's like you have had enough, so you seek help and that's when we come to the rescue.
